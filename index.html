01 / 06 Mission

Make the world computable.

Intelligence, capital, and trust are what a civilization runs on — and each is still allocated by habit, by intuition, by whoever moved first.

This is not an execution problem that scale will fix; it is the absence of a model. We build the one thing that closes the gap — systems that state each problem as a computation, so the better answer wins, not the louder one.

We are not investors who use technology, nor researchers who happen to trade. We write reality as equations — and let the equations decide.

02 / 06 Architecture

We do not do three things. We do one thing — across three dimensions.

The core is single — reduce a complex system to a computation. Intelligence, capital, and trust are not three businesses but three projections of that core; because the core is shared, an advance in one is an advance in all.

Substrate I Intelligence Artificial Intelligence II Capital Quantitative Finance III Trust Blockchain
The question Can cognition be produced at scale? How should scarce capital be allocated? How do strangers coordinate without a middleman?
Form Research forged directly into production systems. Bearing and pricing risk with our own and entrusted capital. Building the protocols and rails, not merely using them.
Domain The unsolved problems inside others' production. A-shares, US equities, crypto — markets that never sleep. A global network across chains, institutions, and borders.
Toward Intelligence as public infrastructure — dispatchable on demand. Every unit of risk earning exactly what it is owed — no more. Value crossing borders as freely as information.

Intelligence is the method; capital, the proving ground; trust, the rail. The three are not a portfolio — they are one engine, and it compounds.

05 / 06 The Method

A method is an algorithm applied to ourselves.

Six steps, applied in order to every problem we touch — the discipline shared beneath all three dimensions.

01

Reduce to the computable

Any problem that cannot be stated as a computation is first restated until it can.

02

Delete the unfalsifiable

An assumption no data can refute carries no value. Remove it.

03

Minimise parameters

Every added degree of freedom is paid for in overfitting.

04

Let the data speak

Priors yield to evidence. The backtest precedes the intuition.

05

Automate execution

People decide the principles. Systems execute the decisions.

06

Iterate without end

A model is a process, not a conclusion. The boundary keeps moving.
